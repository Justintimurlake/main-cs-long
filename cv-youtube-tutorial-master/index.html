About Me

Working as a waiter for me it is unlike any other job. It is a chance to discover new dishes, such as Armenian (Dolma, Kufta, Ton) Georgian (Hachapuri, Lobio, Mamaliga), Russian/Ukrainian (Borsh, Pelmeni, Kompot), East European (Pleskavica, Chevapchichi, Shopsky salate).
